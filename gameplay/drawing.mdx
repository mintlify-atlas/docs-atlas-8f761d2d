---
title: "Drawing"
description: "How to draw on the shared canvas as the active drawer."
---

When it's your turn, you become the active drawer. Your job is to illustrate the chosen word so that the other players can guess it — without typing or saying the word.

## Choosing a word

Before you draw, you must pick a word. The game presents you with a set of word choices (default: 3, configurable by the host). You have **15 seconds** to select one.

<Note>
If you don't pick within 15 seconds, a word is selected for you automatically.
</Note>

Other players see the message "[Your Name] is choosing a word" and a masked placeholder (underscores) that hints at the word length. They do not see the word choices.

## The drawing phase

Once you select a word, the drawing phase starts. You can see the full word at the top of the screen. Everyone else sees only the masked version, for example:

```
_ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _   (elephant)
_ _ _ _ _ _   _ _ _ _   (eiffel tower — spaces are preserved)
```

You have a countdown timer (default: **80 seconds**) visible to all players. Draw as clearly as you can before time runs out.

## Drawing tools

The toolbar appears at the bottom of the canvas when it is your turn. Other players see the canvas in read-only mode.

| Tool | Description |
|---|---|
| **Pen** | Freehand stroke with the selected color and size. |
| **Eraser** | Remove parts of your drawing. |
| **Fill bucket** | Flood-fill a region with the selected color. |
| **Line** | Draw a straight line between two points. |
| **Rectangle** | Draw a hollow rectangle. |
| **Circle** | Draw a hollow ellipse. |

### Brush size

Use the size slider (2 px – 40 px) to adjust the stroke width. The current size is shown next to the slider.

### Color palette

Choose from 18 preset colors or open the color picker for any custom color. Selecting a color while the eraser is active automatically switches back to the pen tool.

### Undo and clear

- **Undo** (`↺`) — removes the last stroke.
- **Clear** (`🗑`) — wipes the entire canvas.

Both actions are synced to all players in real time.

## Real-time sync

Canvas strokes are pushed to all connected players as you draw. Active strokes update approximately every 100 ms; completed strokes are written immediately when you release the mouse or lift your finger.

<Info>
The canvas supports touch input, so you can draw on a mobile device or tablet.
</Info>

## Vote to skip

Any player (including you) can vote to skip the current turn. If enough players vote to skip, the round ends early and moves to the reveal phase. You cannot stop a skip vote once it reaches the threshold.

## What you cannot do

- You cannot type in the chat while you are drawing. The input is disabled and shows "You are drawing..."
- You cannot see other players' guesses in the chat during your drawing turn — this is intentional to prevent you from reacting to close guesses.
