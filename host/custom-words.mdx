---
title: "Custom words"
description: "Add your own words to the drawing pool for a room."
---

Custom words let you tailor the drawing pool to your group. You might add inside jokes, themed vocabulary for a specific event, or anything else you want players to draw.

## Adding custom words

In the **Host Settings** panel (lobby only), click **Custom Words** to expand the input area. Enter your words as a comma-separated list:

```text
pizza, unicorn, Eiffel Tower, submarine, rubber duck
```

You can type as many words as you like. Words are trimmed of leading and trailing whitespace automatically.

<Tip>
Multi-word phrases work fine. "Eiffel Tower" is treated as a single entry and the space is preserved in the word mask shown to guessers.
</Tip>

## How custom words enter the pool

Custom words are mixed into the same pool as words from the selected Firestore word bank categories. When the game fetches word choices for a drawer, it samples from the combined pool. Each custom word is assigned the difficulty label `"custom"`.

The **Word choices** setting (default: 3) determines how many options the drawer sees per turn. Custom words compete alongside category words for those slots — there is no guaranteed frequency.

## Scope and persistence

<Warning>
Custom words are per-room and per-session. They are not saved to the database and will be lost if you refresh the page or start a new game session.
</Warning>

- Custom words apply only to the current room.
- They reset when the host clears the field or starts a new session.
- Other rooms are not affected.

## Examples

| Use case | Example words |
|---|---|
| Food theme | `sushi, croissant, tiramisu, bubble tea` |
| Office party | `spreadsheet, coffee machine, standing desk` |
| Movie night | `plot twist, cliffhanger, slow motion` |
| Geography quiz | `Amazon river, Mount Fuji, Great Barrier Reef` |

<Note>
Words are matched case-insensitively when guessing. "Eiffel Tower", "eiffel tower", and "EIFFEL TOWER" all count as correct.
</Note>
